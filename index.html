AniHyou

AniHyou is your perfect iOS/Android app to track and discover Anime & Manga! You can create lists to organise your series, view user ratings, reviews and official streaming sites. The app is written in Swift with SwiftUI for the iOS version and Kotlin with Jetpack Compose for the Android version. It uses Apollo for the GraphQL API integration. There is also a watchOS version and widgets!

Website

MoeList

MoeList is an unofficial Android MyAnimeList client, with this app you can easily track, rate, organize and discover new Anime & Manga. With more than 90,000 downloads on Play Store, this is my most popular project. Written in Kotlin and using Ktor for the official REST API, the app follows the latest Material Design 3 guidelines.
